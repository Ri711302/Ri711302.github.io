The A-B-C's of Sorting

Alphabetical order is all about following the sequence of the letters in the alphabet, from A to Z. It's a simple skill, but super useful for organizing lists, finding words in a dictionary, or sorting names.

Here's how it works:

  • Look at the first letter of each word.
  • Arrange the words based on the order of these letters in the alphabet.
  • If the first letters are the same, look at the second letter.
  • Keep going until you find a letter that is different.
