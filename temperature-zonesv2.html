Overview

The Earth is divided into different regions based on climate and temperature. These regions are known as Temperature Zones. They are caused by the way the Earth receives sunlight due to its spherical shape and tilt of the axis.

Because sunlight does not fall equally on all parts of the Earth, some regions are very hot, some are moderately warm, and others are very cold.
