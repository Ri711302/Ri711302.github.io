Fascinating Solar System Facts

Discover the wonders of our cosmic neighborhood!

🌍

Water Beyond Earth

Earth isn’t the only planet with water. Scientists have found evidence of water ice on the Moon, Mars, and even some moons of Jupiter and Saturn.

🌞

The Sun’s Massive Size

The Sun makes up about 99.8% of the total mass of the Solar System. Everything else—planets, moons, asteroids—fits into the tiny leftover!

🪐

Jupiter, Our Protector

Jupiter’s strong gravity often pulls in comets and asteroids, preventing them from hitting Earth.

🌙

The Drifting Moon

Each year, the Moon moves about 3.8 cm farther from Earth.

🔴

Mars’ Giant Volcano

Olympus Mons on Mars is nearly three times taller than Mount Everest.

💨

Venus, the Hottest Planet

Even though Mercury is closer to the Sun, Venus has thick clouds of carbon dioxide that trap heat, making it the hottest planet.

🌀

Saturn Could Float

Saturn is mostly made of gas and is so light for its size that it would float if you could find a bathtub big enough!

🌀

Massive Storms

Jupiter’s “Great Red Spot” is a storm that has been raging for over 300 years and is larger than Earth itself.

🌌

A Vast Solar System

If the Sun were the size of a front door, Earth would be the size of a coin—and about 25 meters away.

☄️

Cosmic Snowballs

Comets are made of ice, dust, and rock, and when they get close to the Sun, they grow a glowing tail.
