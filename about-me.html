About Me

Rita Enemuwe (BSc, MBA, TRCN, MCE) has over two decades of teaching experience. She integrates hands-on activities, technology, and etiquette in her classroom to provide students with a fun yet meaningful education, while preparing them for the world they live in. Passionate about teaching, she continually explores new and creative ways to help children learn. In addition to teaching, she designs curricula and educational banners. Beyond the classroom, her hobbies include graphic design, photography, and sewing.
