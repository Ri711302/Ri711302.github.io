About Me

Hi! I’m Rita Enemuwe and I’m glad you’re here. I’m a Microsoft Certified Educator and a Microsoft Innovative Educator Expert with a Bachelor’s and a Master’s degree and I’m also a TRCN-certified and licensed professional. With over two decades of teaching experience, I integrate hands-on activities, technology and etiquette in my classroom to provide students with a fun and meaningful education while preparing them for the world they live in. Teaching is my passion and I continually explore new and creative ways to help children learn. I also create instructional charts and banners.
